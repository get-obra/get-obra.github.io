What Obra does

A reservation, attended to.

A small vacation rental host in Portugal. She doesn't speak English. Portuguese law requires her to collect every guest's passport and report it to the police within 24 hours of arrival. Today she manages this with translated emails that read as cold, missed deadlines that risk fines, and a hundred and fifty euros a month to an accountant just to handle her invoices.

When a booking arrives, Obra picks up the work. Routine steps just get done. Anything that touches the regulator, the guest's money, or her reputation lands in her hands for sign-off first.

01
Pre-arrival
Drafts the welcome email in the guest's native language, in the host's voice. Warm. Hospitable. Clear about what Portuguese law requires. The host approves and it sends.
02
Passport collection
Reads the passport scan the guest sends back. A second independent read cross-checks the numbers. The host signs off. The report lands on the police portal on time.
03
During the stay
Answers questions in the guest's language. The wifi password. The best restaurant nearby. Where to drop the keys. Escalates only when judgment is actually needed.
04
After check-out
Schedules the cleaning crew. Drafts the thank-you in the right language for the right day. Drafts the review request that drives the five-star rating. The host signs off on what reaches the guest.
05
Month end
Generates the invoice. The host approves the figures. Hands a clean record to the accountant for sign-off. The books close. No scramble.
06
The receipt
Every step lands in a cryptographic audit trail the host controls entirely. Who did what, when, and why. Exportable for the tax authority, the data regulator, or her own peace of mind.
The host sleeps through the night before a check-in. The guest never knows their welcome came from anyone but her.

How Obra is built

Trust, by construction.

Obra runs on your infrastructure, invoking Claude under your own API key. Every action passes through three independent gates: structured reasoning, adversarial review, and human confirmation. Each gate lands in a cryptographic audit chain you control entirely. Your data never crosses to us. The chain is designed to be witnessed by independent third parties so any auditor, regulator, or future you can verify what happened, when, and why.

Operator-blind
We never see your data — provable from the schema, not from a policy document. You control the AI relationship directly, under your own API key.
Reads must agree
Two independent reads of critical documents must agree before any value enters review. Disagreement halts and lands in your hands.
Audit you control
Every action signed and replayable. Export on demand for the tax authority, the data regulator, or your own records.
No horror story
Three independent gates on every gated action. The AI proposes. A red team challenges. A human confirms. The action your business takes is the one you approved.
